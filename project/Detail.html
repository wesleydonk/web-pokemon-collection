Maker & story

M
Maya R.Lisbon, PT · making since 2024 · 26 of 151

I started Bulbasaur on a rainy weekend with a half-used skein of sage green I'd been saving. The bulb was the fiddly part — I frogged the increase rounds twice before the shaping sat right on the back.

The seed-stitch texture on the bulb is what I'm proudest of. If you're making your first one, do the body and bulb separately and seam at the end — much easier than working them as one piece.

Notes & mods

  • Worked body & bulb separately, then ladder-stitched the bulb on — far cleaner than the as-one-piece version in the pattern.
  • Embroidered the eye outline in near-black rather than felt; holds shape better over time.
  • Under-stuffed the feet so it sits flat and doesn't topple forward.
