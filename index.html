Why it's designed this way

The grid is the product

Everything orbits one canonical grid of 151. The slow fill from faint placeholders to colored makes is the core emotional payoff, so it's the home screen and never buried behind navigation.

Editorial, not gamified

Crisp neutrals, generous whitespace, a single soft-violet accent, and mono labels for craft specs. It reads like a maker's catalogue — calm and grown-up — rather than a badge-grinding app.

Two-tap logging

The make is captured straight off the hook: only the creature and one photo are required; pattern, yarn, and colors are encouraged but never block the save. Drafts autosave.

State carried beyond color

Made vs. to-make uses a check icon, a label, and a dashed border — not hue alone — so the collection is legible in both themes and to color-blind makers.
